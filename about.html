Purpose of website "BlueBasics Gear"

A simple yet useful website athat consist of steps, tutorials, and functions related to the SCUBA Diving Gear.

The goal of making the website is to let people know the importance of learning steps of installing SCUBA Diving Gear as well as get to know the functions of each equipment, mainly learning about the technology. Furthermore, learning how to install the SCUBA gear will help those who want to do self-check the gear's condition, and people could also know the certains action that is needed to be done for certain situations. In addition, BlueGear Basics also made in order to promote awareness regarding how interesting the learning process of scuba diving is.

Creator's life background

Papua Island, the part of the Indonesian eastern region, is the region that is more devolved with natural resources, great biodiversity, and known for its conservation designation.

My parents were having their business right on Papua Island, my two siblings and I were raised there. Personally, as someone who grew up in Papua Island, which is one of the islands in Indonesia that is known for its ecosystem diversity, including both marine and forest, I was amazed and found nature to be the only entertainment that I would always seek. In particular, I deeply love the ocean and have always searched for great places to swim together with my family members.

The fondness towards the ocean has been proceeding even deeper and led me to the eagerness in experiencing scuba diving. Unexpectedly, during my 2024 summer break, I went back to my hometown. It was the time when I did try scuba diving and swam up to a depth of 20 m. I could say that scuba diving was such a surreal experience, seeing that I was able to view the underwater world as well as acknowledge the various types and looks of fish.

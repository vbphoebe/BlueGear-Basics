SCUBA
Dive Gear

SCUBA Dive Gear is a set of equipment that is used to promote the convenience in carrying out the underwater activity. It is capable of helping the diver to move freely in water, even when they are descending, ascending, or floating. Scuba diving gear is sure to increase the level of safety during diving. This is mainly because of the fact that it is involved in a large quantity of equipment necessary for use in scuba diving from head to toe.

The scuba gear itself generally includes a dive mask, snorkel, scuba kits, and fins. In particular, the scuba kits are divided into 4 parts such as BCD (Buoyancy Control Device), regulator, cylinder or tanks, and weight system. Other than that, there is more diving equipment needed for the scuba diving activity. This includes SPG, dive computers, and signaling devices.
